About Us

Spitfire was formed in 2018 by four singers from the founding chorus of the Harmony STL Chapter, the Ambassadors of Harmony. Spitfire won the Central States District Quartet Contest in 2019 and, due to unforeseen circumstances, held that title for three years. They have had the wonderful fortune of competing in the last four Barbershop Harmony Society International Competitions (2019, 2022, 2023, and 2024) and performing for audiences across the country.

The quartet is composed of:

  • Austin Veteto (tenor)
  • Chris Pingel (lead)
  • Stephen Vandenberg (baritone)
  • JT Reid (bass)

They enjoy singing a variety of genres of music and display, as some have called it, "big dad energy."
